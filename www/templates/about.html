Siyum Daily is an exciting new program. Hundreds of people will become partners in completing an entire section of the Torah in a single day!

We will begin with תהלים and ששה סדרי משנה. When you sign up for Siyum Daily, we will assign you one Mishnah and/or פּסוקים from תהלים.

Thousands of people will each learn their own משנה or תהלים, making a siyum תהלים and משניות in just one day. After a few days, we’ll assign you a different משנה or תהלים. We’ll keep doing this day after day – so all of us collectively will be making a Siyum every single day.

Later we’ll do the same with other parts of our vast Torah. Siyuim after Siyum! Day after day! So we’ll all be partners in a daily Kiddush Hashem many time over.

Do you want to be part of it? Of course you do! Recruit your friends.

About us

Yeshiva Ateres Shimon is located in Far Rockaway NY. The yeshiva has a student body of more then 200, who are young working men ages 17- 45 and up. There are many different groups, shiurim and study partners that come learn in the Yeshiva throughout the day. The yeshiva’s early morning learning program starts at 5:45 am and our evening session late night learning goes as late as 11:00pm.

We have 15 email-based learning programs that began 4 years ago with 25 people – and now, Baruch Hashem, Ateres Shimon’s email “yeshiva” has over 21,000 daily subscribers. We pray to Hashem for siyata diShmaya to continue being vehicles to glorify His great Name.
